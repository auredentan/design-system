Dialogs inform users about a task and can contain critical information, require decisions, or involve multiple tasks.

Simple dialogs display a list of items that take immediate effect when selected.

Alert dialogs interrupt users with urgent information, details, or actions.

Confirmation dialogs require users to confirm a choice before the dialog is dismissed.

Most dialog content should avoid scrolling. When scrolling is required, the dialog title is pinned at the top, with buttons pinned at the bottom.

Dialogs comes in four optional sizes: tiny, regular, big, and huge.
